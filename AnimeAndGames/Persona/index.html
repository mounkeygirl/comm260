Persona is a Japanese Role Playing Game (JRPG) where the player fights monsters using their Persona, and strengthens this ability by interacting with other charactes in the story. It's something of a spin off of the Megami Tensei universe, and has gotten a rather large following in the USA since the third game in the series. There are currently core five games.

The Megami Tensei games tend to lean on a demon-summoning game mechanic, although for the Persona games the player is unlocking parts of his own potential. How exactly varies on the game.

There are also a large number of spin off games, including rythem games and more traditional arcade style fighting games.
